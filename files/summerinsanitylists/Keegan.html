Selection Rules

Armourbane: Additional D6 armor penetration. (BRB 2014 p157)

Combined Squad: May combine Infantry Squads just before rolling for Warlord Traits. (Codex: Astra Militarum)

Crawler: Not affected by DIfficult or Dangerous terrain. Cannot Run. (Codex: Skitarii p63)

Doctrina Imperatives: Select one at beginning of your Movement phase to last until your next turn. Each is One Use Only.

Fear: Unit in base contact must pass Fear test at beginning of each Fight sub-phase or fight at Weapon Skill 1. (BRB 2014 p163)

Feel No Pain (6+): (BRB 2014 p164)

Field Harmonics: +1 invulnerable save for each squadmate within 4" (Codex: Skitarii p63)

Gets Hot: Firing model takes wound for each 1 rolled To Hit. Cannot Look Out, Sir. Vehicles suffer glancing hit after failing additional 4+ save. (BRB 2014 p164)

Ignores Cover: No cover saves for these wounds, glancing hits, or penetrating hits (BRB 2014 p165)

Interceptor: After enemy Movement, may fire at unit just arriving from Reserve. Then cannot fire this weapon next turn. (BRB 2014 p167)

Invincible Behemoth: (40k Apocalypse 2nd Ed p66)

Move Through Cover: Roll extra D6 for difficult terrain. Automatically pass Dangerous Terrain. (BRB 2014 p168)

Rad Poisoning: Each 6 rolled To Wound causes 2 Wounds, no matter target's Toughness. (Codex: Skitarii p74)

Rad-saturation: Enemy units locked in combat with any number of models with this rule, subtract 1 from their Toughness (but no lower than 1). (Codex: Skitarii p57)

Relentless: Model may shoot Ordnance, Salvo, or Heavy weapons after moving as if they were stationary. Model may also charge after firing Rapid Fire, Salvo, Ordnance, or Heavy weapons. (BRB 2014 p170)

Skyfire: Shoots full Ballistic Skill at Skimmers, Flying Monstrous Creatures, and Flyers. Snap Shots only at other targets. (BRB 2014 p171)

Sniper: Always wounds any Toughness on 4+ roll To Wound. Rolls of 6 To Wound become AP2. Against vehicles, instead counts as Strength 4. Also has Precision Shots for any 6 rolled To Hit. (BRB 2014 p171)
